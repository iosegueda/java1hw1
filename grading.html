INSY 3300/5309 Grading Policy

HW Turn in Format

  1. You should submit your assignments electronically on the Blackboard by the specified due date and time. No late submissions will be accepted.
  2. Turn in ONLY the .java files and any other additional files specified in the assignment.
  3. Your programs should compile and run from the command prompt the way it is showed in the class lectures.
  4. Make sure to write your full name as a comment at the very beginning of all the .java files.
  5. You are only allowed one submission, so please double check your files before you submit.

Grading Policy

  1. If your program does not compile, you will not get more than 20% of the maximum points.
  2. You will get the maximum points only if your program compiles properly and executes according to the requirements specified in the assignment.
